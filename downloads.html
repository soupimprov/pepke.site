    Cello Jam and Viola Jam

      This book series aims to help classical string players feel more comfortable in popular styles. The work is a development of what I learned in my experience jamming with students and teachers at National Cello Institute in Pomona, CA.

    Concepts covered in this book:

    • How to read and interpret a “lead sheet” in a variety of styles: from swing and samba to baroque!
    • How to play in a jam session and improvise as a group with other musicians
    • How to interpret chord symbols to play accompaniments and bass lines.
    • How to use accents and rhythmic displacement to spice up a melody and create unique phrasing
    • How to analyze harmonic progressions for improvisation

      The books consist of exercises, lead sheets, and stylized arangements of classical tunes with modern and jazzy twists. It is free to download and share, but can not be sold or used commercially (CC BY-NC 4.0). You can learn more about this type of creative commons license here.
